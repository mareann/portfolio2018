FoodSavr App

Full Stack.

  • **FoodSavr is a full stack web app created using MySQL, Node, Express, Handlebars, Sequelize and the MVC (Model View Controller) design pattern!**

  • Main Goal: **Food Waste Reduction Through Donation** Match charities to available food donations
  • TwitterBot. If you visit our Twitter page and tweet @Food_Savr https://twitter.com/Food_Savr , you will receive a thank you response and a random fact about food waste.
  • *Restaurants, grocery stores, and farms who have excess food left over at the end of the day can donate it to nearby charities.*
  • *Charities can register to accept food donations and donors can register to give food donations on this site.*
  • Team Role
    • Database model Sequelize
    • Database seed
    • Load donations/donators/charities into database
    • Google Distance API
    • Research local food donators
    • Research local charities
